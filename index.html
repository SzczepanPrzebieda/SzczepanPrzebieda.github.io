Helpdesk is a crucial section of the Tallaght Hospital which main job is to log problems that are currently occurring in the are of the hospital and distribute them across members working in the ICT department. While working in the Tallaght hospital each day number of tasks were assigned to each technician based on the priority of the issue. The tasks had to be completed as soon as possible and in an efficient manner. The range of calls were wide which started from basic single user problems but also much more advance issues like changing the server information that was affecting number of departments at the same time. Huge precision and details were needed to complete each of the tasks.

Infrastructure is another section of the Tallaght Hospital that is very important and plays a huge role in every day’s tasks completed by the other staff of the hospital. The main role of staff working in the Infrastructure department would be to make changes to the servers that are running for the hospital. Networking staff is also located in the infrastructure section. This section is responsible for all changes that are affecting the hospital as well as introducing new technology and all backup tasks. People that are working in the infrastructure department are also required to have appropriate administrative rights and person that is responsible for the user right distribution.
